Sniv Gantt Chart

  1. Goal: display all the snivs of a set of resources over time
  2. This should have the resources listed as rows and time period horizontally. At its most basic, it could be day/date across the top with unavailable waves as the cell entries. This is what we used last year. On hover, the times and reason should be displayed.
  3. A more sophisticated version could have a visual depiction of available each day directly tied to the times of the sniv. The default availability (from an hour before sunrise to an hour after sunset) would be a green bar and and unavailability would be displayed as a red bar over the green. Again, on hover the times and reason should be displayed.
  4. This could involved showing a large number of days. As someone scrolls, the resource names should stay visible on the left.
  5. For planes, this should display the hours as well.
